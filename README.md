# Privacy Policy for REN_Note

**Effective Date: June 1, 2026**

REN_Note ("we," "our," or "us") is dedicated to protecting your absolute privacy. This Privacy Policy details our privacy practices regarding our mobile application **REN_Note** (the "App").

### 1. 100% Offline Architecture & No Data Collection
REN_Note is built upon a strict offline architecture. 
* **No Server Storage**: All notebooks, entries, checklists, customized themes, and media attachments (photos/videos) are stored strictly and locally on your physical device.
* **No Personal Data Harvesting**: We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, identifiers, accounts, or tracking coordinates on external servers.
* **No Third-Party Analytics**: We do not integrate data trackers, marketing pixels, or advertising analytics SDKs, establishing an absolute tracking-free environment.

### 2. Device Permissions Usage
The App requests specific native system permissions to ensure core features function properly:
* **Storage / Files Access**: Required to let users pick and attach media files (photos/videos) to their local notes, save encrypted anti-loss backups to system Documents folders, and import JSON configuration backups.
* **Biometric Hardware Protection**: Used to interface with the device's native fingerprint scanner (Touch ID / Secure Enclave) locally to unlock private notebooks. All biometric credential validation is executed entirely by your device's native trust zone. We have zero access to your actual fingerprint data.

### 3. In-App Purchases (IAP) Billing Data
All tipping donations are processed through the official Google Play Billing system. Billing transactions, currency checks, and merchant processing are handled directly by Google. REN_Note has zero access to your credit card data, bank information, or transactional logs.

### 4. Children's Privacy
Because we collect absolutely zero personal information from any user, REN_Note is 100% compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and does not harvest data from children under the age of 13.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we have no contact channels or server databases, all updates will be posted directly within this page.
